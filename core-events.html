SEGUIDOR

Quasso Liberum 8 presents SEGUIDOR Line follower , the most popular robotics competition in Indian campuses. Design a line follower robot most efficiently to complete maximum checkpoints in the least time and win the competition.

Prizes worth 5k awaits you...

Date : 21/03/19
Time : 03:00 PM

Registration Fees: ₹300 per team

RULES

1. Size - 20*20*20 cm(max) Power supply - 12v - 15v.
2. Maximum members in a group – 4.
3. Cross college teams are not allowed.
4. If bot remains immobile for respectable time, then on the spot decision will be taken by the organisers.
5. Bot has to follow line on surface.
6. Width of the line - 3 cm.
7. Length of line - Based on level.
8. There shall be cross over.
9. Switch back and hair pin are possible.
10. Curve radius - 7.5 cm to 10 cm.
11.Right angle, acute angle, obtuse angle, loops, peaks may occur.
12.Each touch leads to 10 second penalty.
13.There will not be any skip in first round.
14.Maximum of 4 skips will be allowed in second and third rounds.
15.Penalty for skipping will be based on the portion skipped.

         This competiton is a unique race testing the ingenuity assembly skills and understanding of pratical problems by the participants. There will be new and more complex challenges added to the competition. The participants are expected to bring a working model of a craft to the venue that is designed to cross a length of water. The competion will be judged on the basis of speed in crossing the length of water.

Maximum members allowed in a group : 4

Prizes worth 6k awaits you...

Date : 21/03/19
Time : 03:00 PM

Registration Fees: 400

RULES

1. Wired/Wireless device is allowed.
2. Route will be disclosed on the day of event.
3. Check points are available between some obstacles. You can skip certain obstacles to the next check point by getting minuspoints.
4. The boat must completely float above the water.(Submarine boat are not entertained).
5. In case of wired boat the wire must not be used to manipulate the boat’s movement.
6. Maximum device dimension: 300mm x 200mm x 200mm (lxbxh).
7. Combustion engines, per-pressurized containers or other fluids must NOT be used.
8. Maximum acceptable power supply: 24V, 5A DC (At any point during the competition).
9. Minimum length of wire (if remote is wired) : 3m
10. The body of the craft must be constructed by the participants themselves and must not be from any prefabricated kit, commercially available toy or a partly assembled factory made kit.
11. Batteries of any strength and type will be allowed.
12. All motors and propellers are allowed.
13. A team/individual can present only one boat.
14. Any part of the device must not be detachable.
15. Any explosive/chemicals are prohibited.
16. Maximum of 3 touches will be provided.
17. Perfomance will be judged based on minimum time taken to complete the track with minimum skips and touches.
18. Above mentioned rules are subject to modification without prior notice to the group on any time before the event.
