Quasso Liberum is the techno-cultural fest of College of Engineering, Vadakara. It also refers to the independent body of students who organize this event along with many other social initiatives and outreach programs round the year. Techfest is known for hosting a variety of events that include competitions, exhibitions, lectures as well as workshops. Quasso Liberum comprises of 70% - Technical events 30% - Cultural events.
